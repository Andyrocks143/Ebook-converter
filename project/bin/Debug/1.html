1. NEED FOR THE STUDY

The main aspect of this project is to identify and understand the challenges involved in starting a small business and providing solutions for overcoming these obstacles. Identifying these challenges and recommending various methods and ideas to overcome these obstacles will lead an entrepreneur to be well prepared as each and every move made by an entrepreneur is a step closer to success or failure.

2. REVIEW OF LITERATURE

This abstract is to create awareness about the problems faced in starting a small business. It examines the background and challenges faced  by small enterprises which play a vital role in the nation’s growth. Many challenges are encountered while starting a small business as it is majorly started by an individual or individuals with no or minimum experience and limited capital and resources. The challenges range from coming up with an idea for a business to selling the product/service to the customer. Each and every step involved in the process of starting a small business is very important as it can affect the success of the company- International Journal of Business and Management.

3.OBJECTIVE

The main objective of this project is to identify the challenges faced in starting a small business.

(ii)SECONDARY OBJECTIVE

The secondary objective is to recommend and suggest suitable solutions to overcome the challenges.
